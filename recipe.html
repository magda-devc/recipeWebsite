Pilau recipe

Ingredients

- 2 cups of rice
- 5 potatoes medium size [peeled and cut into quarters]
- ¼ cup vegetable oil
- 2 Red onions medium size [thinly sliced]
- 1 dicely chopped green pepper
- 2 tablespoons garlic minced
- 2 tablespoons ginger minced
- 1 tablespoon pilau masala
- ½ kg of cubed meat
- 3 tomatoes thinly chopped
- 4 cups water
- 1 packet of tomato paste
- 1 teaspoon of grounded rosemary leaves
- Salt to taste

Instructions

First boil the meat(beef or any other type of meat) until nicely cooked.

  1. Heat a non-stick pot on medium-high heat. Add oil and heat until hot but not smoking.
  2. Add the sliced onions and fry for 10-15 minutes or till the onions starts to turn golden brown (be careful not to burn it).
  3. Add the garlic, ginger, green pepper, Cook for 1-2 minutes.
  4. Add tomato paste and cook for 1 minute until it turns brown.
  5. Add the cubed boiled meat, pilau masala and salt to taste. Cook until the meat browns and caramelizes stirring occasionally about 8 to 10 minutes.
  6. Add the diced tomatoes and cook for 4-5 minutes, until the tomatoes have released their liquid.
  7. Stir in the potatoes and the water. Bring to a boil and cook for 10 minutes or until the potatoes are cooked.
  8. Stir in the rice,add 3 cups of water and cover (to keep the steam in).
  9. Reduce the heat to low and cook for 20 minutes, until the rice is cooked and the liquid is absorbed.
  10. Once the rice is dry, fluff with a fork to ensure the seasonings are well distributed all around the rice.

Serve hot with fresh Kachumbari. Enjoy!
